About Little Lemon

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. The restaurant was founded by two Italian-Greek brothers, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of opening a restaurant.

Our menu features a variety of dishes inspired by the flavors of the Mediterranean, with a focus on fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. From our signature dishes like Moussaka and Spanakopita, to our daily specials and seasonal offerings, we strive to provide our guests with a unique and memorable dining experience.
